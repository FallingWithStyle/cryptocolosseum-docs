The total supply is 750,000,000 PTG.
* 100,000,000 PTG for the DAO to operate (~ 4mm spent on migrating testnet users to mainnet).
    * The DAO's mission is to bring in more players to the Arena and to make the Crypto Colosseum more fun.
* 50,000,000 PTG for new user incentives. Over time, this number will decrease.
* 300,000,000 PTG for tournament incentives. Over the next 4 years tournament betting pools will be incentivized with additional PTG. Monthly's get the most, then weeklies then dailies.
* 300,000,000 PTG for liquidity incentives.
